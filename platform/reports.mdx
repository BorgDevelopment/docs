Reports give you a structured summary of your security posture, useful for internal reviews, compliance, or sharing with stakeholders.

## Available report types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pentest report" icon="shield-check">
    A full write-up of a specific Mjolnir run, including findings, reproduction steps, severity ratings, and suggested fixes. Generated automatically at the end of each run.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Findings summary" icon="chart-bar">
    A snapshot of all open findings across your workspace, grouped by severity. Useful for executive briefings and compliance reviews.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What's in a pentest report

Each pentest report includes:

- **Executive summary**: high-level overview of the assessment scope, methodology, and key results
- **Findings breakdown**: every confirmed vulnerability with severity, description, reproduction steps, and suggested fix
- **Risk summary**: an aggregated view of risk by severity and category
- **Scope details**: the target URLs, repositories, and test users used during the run
- **Methodology**: how Mjolnir performed the assessment (reconnaissance, attack surface mapping, test case generation, execution)

## Exporting

Reports can be exported as PDF. Navigate to **Management > Reports**, select the report you want, and click **Export**.

## Scheduled reports

<Note>
  Scheduled reports are available on the Business plan.
</Note>

Business plan customers receive a monthly email report summarising:

- New findings discovered since the last report
- Remediation progress across open findings
- Attack surface changes (new domains, subdomains, or endpoints discovered)
- A comparison of your security posture month over month
