## How it works

Odin is organised around your **workspace**, a container for your organisation's assets, findings, and pentest history. When you log in, you land on the Home dashboard showing your current security posture at a glance.

The sidebar gives you access to everything:

- **General**: home, alerts, and a global findings view
- **Attack Surface**: assets, map, and activity feed
- **Pentests**: Mjolnir (AI whitebox), Black Box, and Triage
- **Management**: workflows, integrations, reports, and team settings

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Add your name and photo so your team knows who's who.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your findings">
    Head to **Findings** to see your current security posture.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your attack surface">
    Go to **Assets** to see what's been discovered about your organisation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run your first AI pentest">
    Navigate to **Pentests > Mjolnir** and follow the setup wizard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
